About Us

Who Are We

Al Khazen Software Center was established to develop Al Khazen Accounting and Warehouse System. The development went through multiple stages, where the possibility of dealing in multiple currencies and the branch feature (which operate according to the principle of holding and subsidiary companies) were added. A wide range of accounting and warehouse analytical reports were added to the system, in addition to supporting work within computer networks and managing user permissions. In it, the Microsoft SQL Server database system was adopted in most of its versions to store system information, and the system became capable of dealing with a huge volume of data and a large number of users, and the system was developed to support various control procedures for accounting operations, in addition to generating detailed and analytical reports. Which facilitates the task of accountants and helps managers make decisions while maintaining the confidentiality of data.
The system has been well received by accountants and decision makers because of the reliability, speed of performance, and flexibility of work it provides.

Al Khazen Software Center has been working on developing accounting systems that are compatible with the requirements of the labor market and its rapid and continuous developments.
The center aims to support the modern business environment by developing accounting and administrative systems according to the latest methods in the field of software, and providing accounting consultations to improve companies’ performance for the better.
The center develops Al Khazen system for accounting and warehouses, and implements specialized independent software according to companies’ requirements while providing the possibility of linking it with Al Khazen system, in addition to providing a variety of accounting services and consultations for companies.

Products

Softwares That We Provide

  • Al-Khazen.Net

    It is a system for controlling accounting and warehousing work that meets the requirements of the modern administrative and accounting work environment for large, medium and small-sized companies, starting from recording operations and then posting them (supporting the various control procedures for these operations), to generating detailed and analytical reports that facilitate the task of accountants and help managers make decisions while maintaining the confidentiality of the data.
    Al-Khazen.net is based on the Microsoft SQL Server system within the Windows operating environment, which is characterized by speed, reliability and optimum performance for long periods of operation.
    The system came in its current version as a result of a series of painstaking work since 1988 and after a series of previous versions starting with version 1 and ending with the current version where the experiences gained from all these versions were poured into the new version of our accounting systems Al-Khazen.net.

  • Al-Khazen Lite

    It is a simplified version of Al-Khazen Accounting and Warehousing System directed to small-sized projects and meets the requirements of a simple work environment and supports various accounting operations (from receipt and payment) in addition to managing multiple warehouse operations.
    Al-Khazen Lite offers multiple features that suit the simple nature of work, and the most prominent of these features are:

    • Daily recording of all receipts and payments.
    • Statements of balances of customers and suppliers.
    • Reports on account balances and fund movement.
    • Record all types of invoices.
    • Controlling transfers between warehouses.
    • Detailed reports on the movement of items.
    • Determine the profit of each item of the invoice when selling and the profit of the invoice as a whole.
    • Warehouse inventory in different ways.
    • POS and barcodes.
    • Preparing the income statement, the statement of financial position and the final budget.

  • Al-Khazen for Distribution

    The distribution system provides statistical and aggregate reports that enable marketing and sales managers to set marketing plans, policies and sales targets that are entered into the system to control and monitor the implementation of these policies and objectives to the fullest extent.
    The importance of the distribution system is reflected in the ability to take note of the smallest details of delegates’ movement, as the system keeps all the details of delegates’ movements and timelines for their movements from the moment of leaving the company through entering and exiting customers’ visits until returning and completing its daily tour.

    Ease of Calculating Delegates’ Commissions:

    The program provides a flexible method for calculating commissions. This method depends on the distributor achieving a goal or a set of goals, or covering a certain amount of sales on a group of customers, implementing the required activities, and evaluating the sales manager. In addition to the possibility of assigning points for each representative so that the delegate’s points are collected by knowing the goals that have been achieved and their fragmentation on the segments system that makes the commission progressive and commensurate with the work of the distributor, to enhance the productivity of distributors and encourage them to achieve more work.

    Studying The Market Potential for Sales Promotion:

    The system is based on providing integrated solutions for distribution companies, as it provides users with important reports to study market changes, whereby the marketing department will be able to develop successful plans for marketing, starting from dividing the market into channels, distributing customers in each channel to distributors, and following up on the actual coverage of representatives on distribution lines, with the possibility of studying the space occupied by the company's products from the rest of the products in the market and discovering weaknesses in the previous distribution plan, which helps the marketing department to rebuild the distribution plan in a way that ensures better results.

    Archiving Customer Data:

    The data of all customers is archived through a hierarchical structure and the classification of customers into groups (large customers - small points of sale ... etc.) and their distribution on a set of distribution channels so that each customer is linked to a specific sales representative and determines the accreditation ceiling for this customer, the address of this customer and all his personal data.

    Integrated System of Offers:

    The offers system allows activating the company’s sales in a way that ensures that the marketing department monitors these offers and the way they are achieved, and builds any offer, regardless of the complexity of its conditions, and even achieves a cumulative offer and determines a set of gifts resulting from the realization of the offer. These gifts are either mandatory or optional, and determine the period for which the offer is applied and the degree The priority of this offer, as this offer is linked to the reports in an integrated manner to tighten control over them.

  • Al-Khazen Pocket

    It is a system aimed at controlling and accelerating the work of sales representatives during their tours, achieving control over their workflow and following up their operations, starting from registering customer orders to filling cars, and then entering sales operations, returns and cash payments.
    This system works on various handheld computer systems, and it can be connected to a portable printer to print customer invoices.

  • Al-Khazen for Fixed Assets

    It is a system used to control fixed asset operations in accordance with international accounting standards, in addition to controlling covenant operations, as it links the asset to the employee who received it, in addition to linking it to his location to tighten the process of controlling fixed assets.
    Al Khazen Fixed Assets offers multiple features for managing the company’s assets, including:

    • Using the decimal numbering system to encode the assets, so that the system automatically senses the hierarchical level and distinguishes between the main and sub-asset.
    • A record for each asset in which all the asset’s information is recorded in addition to the operations carried out on it: purchase, development, maintenance, depreciation, sale...etc.
    • Classification of assets into various groups, so that each group has special fields and features according to the type of asset.
    • Carrying out all operations on fixed assets: purchase, development, transfer, depreciation, revaluation, etc.
    • Carry out depreciation operations on fixed assets in accordance with international accounting standards: straight, decreasing, increasing, total years..etc.
    • A personal custody system that links the asset to the employee who received it, in addition to linking it to his location to tighten the process of controlling fixed assets.

  • Al-Khazen for HR

    It is a system for managing human resources from archiving employees’ personalities and the development of their job performance to controlling employees’ working hours and vacations and calculating their salaries...etc. The system deals with various equipments for controlling work hours, and it can communicate with Al-Khazen accounting and warehouse system.
    Al-Khazen provides human resources with multiple features that are compatible with the modern work environment, and the most prominent of these features are:

    • Preparing records of employees’ names.
    • Classification of employees according to their departments and job ranks.
    • Determining the actual working days and hours in addition to the official and weekly holidays.
    • The possibility of preparing several models for calculating salaries, additions, bonuses and deductions.
    • Recording the movement of entry and exit of employees.
    • Dealing with fingerprint registration devices and entry and exit cards.
    • The possibility of dividing the day into shifts differs in the models for calculating working hours.
    • The possibility of importing employee timetables from the Excel program.

  • Al-Khazen for Restaurants

    It is a system to control the work of restaurants that operate on the table system and that operate in the system of home delivery in addition to fast food restaurants and cafes, as it eliminates the difficulties in calculating the cost of orders and monitoring the work of supervisors, and provides optimal solutions to all the problems facing the cashier and accountants within the restaurant through a flexible point of sale that allows the registration of all billing, receiving and payment processes easily and a variety of reports.
    The system serves table-style restaurants, home-delivery restaurants, fast-food restaurants, and cafes.
    Al Khazen for Restaurants offers multiple features:

    • Recording orders inside and outside the restaurant, in addition to hospitality and customer reservations.
    • Determine the profit of each item of the order and the profit of the order as a whole.
    • The possibility of merging several tables together.
    • Monitoring the books handed over to supervisors and calculating their commission based on their requests.
    • Editing the order after it has been stored and the possibility of canceling it without affecting the accounts.
    • Control the work of the cashier through the premissions granted to him.
    • Print invoices and adjust fund movement.

  • Al-Khazen for Money Transfers

    Money transfers systems are considered among the most accurate systems as they serve a profession that does not accept error and any software error can cost companies losses that threaten the company’s credibility in the money transfer market. Therefore, the Al-Khazen system for money transfers is considered the first choice for companies as it meets the requirements of this broad profession with the smallest details and simplest interfaces
    Among the features of the Al-Khazen for Money Transfers:

    • Design flexible professional interfaces to receive and deliver money transfers according to the most appropriate form for the nature of this profession.
    • Linking between money transfers and the accounting program to extract the required reports and information.
    • Extensive options for entering money transfers commission values, allowing management to specify commissions for each branch with the ability to set them according to specific segments or according to fixed values.
    • Wide options for distributing the value of commissions between the sending branch, the addressee and the administration according to percentages that can be changed between one branch and another.
    • Generating automatic accounting entries for the receiving branch and the delivered branch, and the commission proofing entries and distributing them among the branches according to the percentages specified by the administration.
    • The ability to send text messages to customers to receive their money transfers, while distributing the value of the messages sent to the branches.
    • A special report designer program that allows the user to design print forms for each report, while allowing the development of a special design for each branch.
    • The ability to enter personal identification information manually or through a special reader.
    • The ability to store a copy of the identity automatically.
    • Flexibility in requesting reports on information about the customer or on internal movements between branch funds.
    • The possibility of determining the time of synchronization of money transfers with each branch separately, which provides the possibility of permanent work on the program even in the event of a connection interruption, as the program re-synchronizes information from the moment of interruption.
    • Full connection with all security authorities regulating the work of money transfer companies, with all required information being sent automatically.
    • Extensive options for managing branches and branch users.
