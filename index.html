About Usمن نحن

Who We Areنبذة عنّا

Al Khazen Software Center was established to develop Al Khazen Accounting and Warehouse System. The development went through multiple stages, where the possibility of dealing in multiple currencies and the branch feature (which operate according to the principle of holding and subsidiary companies) were added. A wide range of accounting and warehouse analytical reports were added to the system, in addition to supporting work within computer networks and managing user permissions. In it, the Microsoft SQL Server database system was adopted in most of its versions to store system information, and the system became capable of dealing with a huge volume of data and a large number of users, and the system was developed to support various control procedures for accounting operations, in addition to generating detailed and analytical reports. Which facilitates the task of accountants and helps managers make decisions while maintaining the confidentiality of data.

The system has been well received by accountants and decision makers because of the reliability, speed of performance, and flexibility of work it provides.

Al Khazen Software Center has been working on developing accounting systems that are compatible with the requirements of the labor market and its rapid and continuous developments. The center aims to support the modern business environment by developing accounting and administrative systems according to the latest methods in the field of software, and providing accounting consultations to improve companies’ performance for the better. The center develops Al Khazen system for accounting and warehouses, and implements specialized independent software according to companies’ requirements while providing the possibility of linking it with Al Khazen system, in addition to providing a variety of accounting services and consultations for companies.

تأسس مركز الخازن للبرمجيات لتطوير نظام الخازن للمحاسبة والمستودعات وقد مر التطوير بمراحل متعددة حيث أضيفت إمكانية التعامل بالعملات المتعددة وميزة الفروع (التي تعمل حسب مبدأ الشركات القابضة والتابعة) وأضيف للنظام مجموعة كبيرة من التقارير التحليلية المحاسبية والمستودعية إضافة إلى دعم العمل ضمن الشبكات الحاسوبية وإدارة صلاحيات المستخدمين فيها، وتم اعتماد نظام قواعد البيانات Microsoft SQL Server بمعظم إصداراته في تخزين معلومات النظام، وأصبح النظام قادر على التعامل مع حجم هائل من البيانات، وعدد كبير من المستخدمين، وتم تطوير النظام داعماً مختلف إجراءات الرقابة الخاصة بالعمليات المحاسبية، إضافة إلى توليد التقارير التفصيلية والتحليلية التي تسهل مهمة المحاسبين وتساعد المدراء على اتخاذ القرارات مع الحفاظ على سرية البيانات.

ولاقى النظام استحسان المحاسبين وصناع القرار لما يوفره من وثوقيه وسرعة في الأداء ومرونة في العمل.

دأب مركز الخازن للبرمجيات في العمل على تطوير نظم محاسبية تتلاءم ومتطلبات سوق العمل وتطوراتها السريعة والمستمرة. يهدف المركز إلى دعم بيئة الأعمال الحديثة من خلال تطوير نظم محاسبية وإدارية وفق أحدث الوسائل ضمن مجال البرمجيات، وتقديم الاستشارات المحاسبية للارتقاء بأداء الشركات نحو الأفضل. يطور المركز نظام الخازن للمحاسبة والمستودعات، وتنفيذ برمجيات مستقلة متخصصة حسب متطلبات الشركات مع توفير إمكانية ربطها مع نظام الخازن، إضافة إلى تقديم مجموعة متنوعة من الخدمات والاستشارات المحاسبية للشركات.

Software We Provideالبرامج التي نقدمها

Al-Khazen.Net

It is a system for controlling accounting and warehousing work that meets the requirements of the modern administrative and accounting work environment for large, medium and small-sized companies, starting from recording operations and then posting them (supporting the various control procedures for these operations), to generating detailed and analytical reports that facilitate the task of accountants and help managers make decisions while maintaining the confidentiality of the data.
Al-Khazen.net is based on the Microsoft SQL Server system within the Windows operating environment, which is characterized by speed, reliability and optimum performance for long periods of operation.
The system came in its current version as a result of a series of painstaking work since 1988 and after a series of previous versions starting with version 1 and ending with the current version where the experiences gained from all these versions were poured into the new version of our accounting systems Al-Khazen.net.

Al-Khazen Lite

It is a simplified version of Al-Khazen Accounting and Warehousing System directed to small-sized projects and meets the requirements of a simple work environment and supports various accounting operations (from receipt and payment) in addition to managing multiple warehouse operations.

Distribution System

The distribution system provides statistical and aggregate reports that enable marketing and sales managers to set marketing plans, policies and sales targets that are entered into the system to control and monitor the implementation of these policies and objectives to the fullest extent.

Al-Khazen Pocket

It is a system aimed at controlling and accelerating the work of sales representatives during their tours, achieving control over their workflow and following up their operations, starting from registering customer orders to filling cars, and then entering sales operations, returns and cash payments.

Fixed Assets

A system to manage fixed assets according to international accounting standards, with tracking, depreciation, and comprehensive asset history.

Human Resources

HR management from employee archives to time attendance, payroll, and integration with Al-Khazen accounting systems.

Restaurants

Restaurant management system for table service, delivery, fast food, and cafés with flexible POS and reporting.

Money Transfers

Secure money transfer system with full compliance, automated accounting entries, and operational controls.
